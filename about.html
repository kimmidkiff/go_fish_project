A Little Bit About Us...

Go Fish Bar & Grill has been servicing the Pinellas County area for over 15 years. We strive to get our seafood fresh from the Gulf, and we are always innovating, both in the kitchen and in the dining room, to give you an underwater experience you aren't soon to forget. Owner Kimberly Midkiff grew up in Pinellas County and has always had a deep love for the ocean, and would like to share that love with you.

Meet the Crew

Head Chef Michael Grouper

Head Chef Michael Grouper has been cooking for over 30 years. His love for cooking seafood started when he spent a summer in Itlay with his Nonna who taught him the traditional ways to cook and prepare fresh seafood. He later went to culinary school at the Culinary Institute of America in New York. Over the years, he has honed his craft and finds joy in experimenting with new recipes. We know you'll love his creations as much as we do.

Front-of-House Manager Amanda Mahi

Front-of-House Manager Amanda Mahi has been working in the service industry for most of her life. She was a server all through college where she got a Masters Degree in business management. Now she uses her knowlege and expertise to lead our staff to be the most customer-oriented in the area. Using her past knowlege as a server and her current knowlege as a manager to service our customers to the very best of her ability.
